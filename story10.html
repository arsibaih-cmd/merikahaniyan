📖 Puri Kahani

🔹 Chapter 1 – The Locked Floor

Ayaan ne kabhi nahi socha tha ki uski life ek normal school trip se horror nightmare ban jayegi.

School trip ke liye unhe ek purane hill hotel me rukna tha. Hotel ka naam tha The Silent Crown. Bahar se building normal lag rahi thi… lekin 3rd floor pe ek board laga tha:

“Floor 3 – Permanently Closed”

Ayaan ka best friend Zaid bola, “Bro, permanently closed ka matlab hota hai andar kuch permanent problem hai.” Sab hans diye. Lekin raat ko 2:17 AM par Ayaan ki aankh khul gayi. Usne clearly suna…

👣 Thak… thak… thak…

Jaise koi wooden floor par dheere dheere chal raha ho. Problem ye thi — unka room 2nd floor par tha. Aur footsteps upar se aa rahe the…

3rd floor se. Jo officially band tha. Ayaan ne himmat karke door khola. Corridor khaali tha. Lekin lift ka button khud-ba-khud blink kar raha tha.

3rd Floor – Selected Lift ka door dheere se khula… Andar sirf ek cheez likhi thi marker se: “Room 307 is still waiting.”

Ayaan ka dil zor se dhadakne laga. Kyuki school list ke hisaab se… Room 307 kisi ko allot hi nahi hua tha. Aur hotel map me… Room 307 exist hi nahi karta tha.

🔹 Chapter 2 – The Third Floor

Ayaan ne Zaid ko zor se hila diya. “Zaid uth! Upar koi chal raha hai!” Zaid ne aankh malte hue kaha, “Bro 3rd floor band hai… tu overthink kar raha hai.” Tabhi…

👣 THAK… THAK… THAK…

Is baar footsteps bilkul clear the. Jaise koi heavy boots pehen kar unke room ke upar seedha chal raha ho. Dono chup ho gaye. Zaid ne dheere se kaha, “Thik hai. Chalte hain. Par saath rahenge.” Dono lift ke paas gaye. Lift ka button kisi ne press nahi kiya tha… phir bhi wo already 3rd floor pe set tha.

Lift ka door dheere se khula. Andar thandi hawa thi. Itni thandi jaise AC nahi… freezer. Lift upar chali. Is baar jab 3rd floor aaya… door khula, par dono bahar nahi nikle.

Corridor pura andhera nahi tha. Emergency lights jal rahi thi — red color ki. Walls pe purane pani ke daag the. Jaise kabhi yahan flood hua ho. Room numbers clearly dikh rahe the:

301 302 303 304 305 306

Aur corridor ke bilkul end pe… Ek extra door. Paint utar chuka tha. Number scratch karke likha gaya tha:307

Zaid ne dheere se bola, “Ye map me nahi tha…” Ayaan ne kaha, “Chal check karte hain. Agar koi prank hai toh expose karenge.” Door locked nahi tha. Sirf aadha khula. Jaise hi unhone dhakka diya… Andar koi furniture nahi tha. Sirf ek purana mirror aur ek kursi.

Mirror me dono ka reflection aa raha tha — is baar Zaid bhi dikh raha tha. “Dekha? Sab normal hai,” Zaid ne thoda confident hoke kaha. Tabhi mirror me reflection thoda delay hone laga. Ayaan ne haath hilaya… reflection ne 2 second baad hilaya. Zaid freeze ho gaya.

Phir mirror ke andar wala Ayaan… muskura diya. Real Ayaan nahi muskura raha tha. Mirror wala Ayaan dheere se bol raha tha… bina sound ke. Zaid ne lip-read karne ki koshish ki. Words the:

“Stay.” Suddenly room ka temperature aur drop ho gaya. Kursi apne aap mirror ke saamne khisak gayi. Door band ho gaya. Andar se lock. Ayaan ne zor lagaya — handle hil raha tha par khul nahi raha. Tabhi unke piche se awaaz aayi…

👣 THAK…

Dono ne dheere se mudh kar dekha. Room me teesra reflection tha. Par room me teesra banda nahi tha. Mirror me koi unke piche khada tha. Face clearly nahi dikh raha tha. Bas aankhen.

Seedha dekh rahi thi. Zaid ka haath kaanpne laga. “Bro… peeche mat dekhna…” Ayaan ne galti kar di. Usne peeche dekh liya. Room bilkul khali tha. Wapas mirror me dekha…

Ab teesra reflection aur paas aa chuka tha. Jaise glass ke bilkul andar se chipka hua ho. Aur usne dheere se haath uthaya. Real world me bhi… Ayaan ke kandhe par kisi ne halka sa touch kiya.

Dono cheekh pade. Light blink hui. Aur sab gayab. Room suddenly normal ho gaya. Mirror bilkul simple.

Door khula. Dono bina piche dekhe bhaag ke lift me ghuse. Lift seedha 2nd floor pe aayi.

🔹 Chapter 3 – Teacher ka Intezaar

Ayaan aur Zaid jab teacher ke room ke bahar pahunchhe, tab raat ke kareeb 2:40 baj rahe the.

Unhone dheere se knock kiya. Tak… tak… Andar se turant awaaz aayi: “Kaun?” Dono ek dusre ko dekhne lage. Ayaan ne kaha, “Ma’am main Ayaan… aur Zaid.” Darwaza turant khul gaya. Teacher jaag rahi thi.

Room ki light already on thi. Jaise woh soyi hi na ho. Ye dekh kar Zaid ne dheere se Ayaan ke kaan me kaha, “Bro… kya ye hamara intezaar kar rahi thi?” Teacher ne un dono ko andar bithaya. Unka chehra thoda tense tha. “Tum dono upar gaye the na?”

Dono shock ho gaye. Ayaan ne poocha, “Ma’am aapko kaise pata?” Teacher ne turant nazar hata li. “Main… main bas guess kar rahi thi.” Room me ek ajeeb si smell thi. Jalne ki. Ayaan ki nazar table par padhi. Wahan ek purani hotel ki file rakhi thi. File khuli hui thi.

Us page par likha tha: “Renovation Incident – Floor 3 – Room 307” Zaid ne poocha, “Ma’am aap ye file kyu dekh rahi thi?”

Teacher ne thoda ruk kar kaha, “Kyuki har saal… jab bhi school trip yahan aati hai… koi na koi baccha 3rd floor ke paas chala jata hai.” Room me achanak light halki si dim hui. Ayaan ne dheere se poocha, “Ma’am… Room 307 me kya hua tha?” Teacher ne gahri saans li.

“10 saal pehle yahan ek caretaker kaam karta tha. Naam tha Raghav. Wo hamesha 3rd floor pe rehta tha. Ek raat renovation ke time fire lagi. Official report me likha hai ki wo andar phas gaya.” Zaid ne kaha, “Body mili?” Teacher ne dheere se sir hila diya. “Nahi.” Room ke kone me rakha mirror halki si hilne laga. Kisi ne use touch nahi kiya tha. Ayaan ka gala sukh gaya.

Teacher ne dheere se kaha, “Log kehte hain Raghav ko hotel band hone ka gussa tha. Usne kaha tha — ‘Yeh kamra kabhi band nahi hoga.’” Tabhi… Thak…

Teacher ke room ke bahar corridor me heavy footsteps ki awaaz aayi. Bilkul waisi hi jaise Ayaan ne pehle suni thi. Teacher ka chehra safed pad gaya. “Ye awaaz… phir se…”

Zaid ne dheere se kaha, “Ma’am… kya aapko bhi ye pehle sunai di hai?” Teacher chup rahi. Phir dheere se boli, “Main har saal sunti hoon.” Achanak door ke niche se halka sa dhuan andar ghusne laga. Jalne ki smell aur tez ho gayi. Mirror me reflection change hua.

Is baar mirror me sirf teen log nahi the. Chautha reflection teacher ke bilkul peeche khada tha. Face jal chuka tha. Skin kaali. Aankhen safed. Ayaan ne hichkichate hue kaha, “Ma’am… peeche mat mudhna…” Teacher ne darr ke maare peeche dekh liya. Room khali. Wapas mirror me dekha… Reflection aur paas aa gaya. Aur is baar usne teacher ke kandhe par haath rakha.

Real world me bhi teacher ke kandhe par jalne ka nishaan ubhar aaya. Teacher cheekh padi. Light band. 5 second andhera. Andhere me sirf ek awaaz: “Room 307… abhi bhi khula hai…” Jab light wapas aayi… Teacher zameen par baithi thi. Kandhe par sach me jalne ka fresh nishaan tha. Darwaza khud-ba-khud dheere se khul gaya. Corridor ke end me…

Lift khuli hui thi. Display blink kar raha tha: 3 Footsteps wapas start ho gaye. Is baar dheere nahi. Tez. Seedha unki taraf aate hue.

🔹 Final Chapter – Jo Kabhi Band Nahi Hua

Teacher ka Sach

Footsteps dheere dheere paas aa rahe the. Teacher ne aankhen band kar li. “Ab chupane ka fayda nahi…” Ayaan aur Zaid dono uske paas baith gaye. Teacher ne halka sa kaanpte hue bolna shuru kiya:

“10 saal pehle… main yahan student thi. Bilkul tumhari tarah school trip par aayi thi.” Room me hawa aur thandi ho gayi. “Hum log bhi curiosity me 3rd floor gaye the. Tab Room 307 officially exist karta tha.” Zaid ne dheere se poocha, “Phir?”

Teacher ki awaaz tootne lagi. “Wahan caretaker Raghav rehta tha. Log kehte the wo thoda alag tha. Hamesha kehta tha — ‘Ye hotel mera hai.’” Ek raat wo hume 307 ke andar le gaya. Usne kaha — “Yahan se koi nahi jaata jab tak main na chahun.”

Teacher ki aankhon me aansu aa gaye. “Fire ka accident nahi tha… wo khud ko band kar chuka tha room me. Aur jab hotel staff ne door toda… room khali tha. Bas jalne ki smell aur mirror par likha tha — ‘Main wapas aunga.’”

Ayaan ka gala sukh gaya. “Ma’am… aap bach kaise gayi?” Teacher ne dheere se jawab diya:

“Main akeli bhaag aayi thi.” Room me achanak mirror crack ho gaya. Ek line khud se likhi: “Jhooth.” Teacher ka chehra safed pad gaya.

🔹 Part 2 – Wapas 3rd Floor

Lift khud hi 2nd floor par aa chuki thi. Door khula. Andar se dhuan nikal raha tha. Is baar teeno saath me ghuse. Lift dheere dheere upar chadhi. 3rd floor par door khula.

Corridor pehle se zyada jal chuka lag raha tha. Walls par kaale haath ke nishaan. Aur corridor ke end par — Room 307. Is baar door pura khula tha. Andar se halki si orange roshni aa rahi thi. Jaise aag jal rahi ho.

Teeno dheere dheere andar gaye. Room ka paint utar chuka tha. Ceiling ka kuch hissa jala hua. Beech me wahi mirror.

Mirror me reflection dikh raha tha… Par is baar reflection present ka nahi tha. Mirror me 10 saal purana scene chal raha tha. Young teacher… aur uske saath ek aur ladka. Zaid ne dheere se kaha, “Ma’am… ye kaun hai?”

Teacher ro padi. “Wo mera dost tha… Arjun.” Mirror me dikha — fire lag rahi hai. Arjun room ke andar phas gaya. Teacher bhaag rahi hai.

Arjun chillata hai — “Mujhe chhod ke mat jao!” Reflection freeze ho gaya. Room me ek jalne ki tez smell phail gayi. Mirror ke andar se awaaz aayi:

“Tum bhaagi thi…” Suddenly mirror ka glass liquid ki tarah hilne laga. Aur usme se ek aadmi bahar nikla. Skin jali hui. Aankhen safed. Caretaker Raghav.

🔹 Part 3 – Ant

Raghav ne seedha teacher ki taraf dekha. “Tum wapas aayi ho.” Teacher piche hati. “Main majboor thi…” Raghav ne haath uthaya. Room ka door band. Zaid ne Ayaan ka haath pakda. Raghav ne teacher ka haath pakda — us jagah jahan jalne ka nishaan tha. Teacher cheekh padi.

Mirror ke andar aag bhadak uthi. Raghav ne dheere se kaha: “Is baar koi akela nahi bhaagega.” Achanak mirror se kaale haath nikle. Ek ne teacher ko khinch liya.

Dusra… Ayaan ki taraf badha. Zaid ne zor se pakda. “Bro mat ja!” Par Ayaan ka haath slippery ho gaya. Jaise kisi ne usse peeche se pakad liya ho.

Ayaan ne last baar Zaid ko dekha. “Mat aana wapas…” Aur wo mirror ke andar kheench liya gaya. Teacher bhi gayab. Room me sirf Zaid reh gaya. Mirror toot kar normal ho gaya. Room wapas khali.

Sirf ek cheez bachi thi. Wall par naya number likha hua: 308

🔚 Epilogue

Subah hotel staff ne report likhi:

“Do log missing.”

3rd floor phir se permanently sealed. Lekin raat ke 3:08 par…

2nd floor ke mirror me kabhi kabhi reflection me teen log khade dikhte hain. Aur ek awaaz aati hai: “Room 307 kabhi band nahi hota…”
