👁️‍🗨️Shraap

By Arslan Hussain

Ek gaon… jahan log marte nahi… chup ho jaate hain Ek ped… jo sirf saaya nahi deta… yaadein rakhta hai Ek bachcha… jo sach bolta hai… aur phir akela chhod diya jaata hai Yeh kahani bhoot ki nahi… yeh kahani logon ki chup ki hai Naam yaad rakhna— SHRAAP

📖 Puri Kahani

🩸 SHRAAP – PART 1

Gaon ke bahar ek bahut purana peepal ka ped tha, jiske baare me koi khulkar baat nahi karta tha.

Din ke waqt woh ped bilkul normal dikhta, jaise har gaon me hota hai. Par jaise hi shaam hoti, log us taraf dekhna bhi chhod dete. Bachchon ko sakht mana tha ki wahan jaakar khelna nahi hai. Jab koi poochta “kyon?”, jawab milta—“bas mana hai.”

Isi gaon me rehta tha Ayaan, ek chupchaap sa ladka jo likhna aur sochna pasand karta tha. Ayaan ko lagta tha ki har mana ke peeche koi wajah hoti hai. Ek shaam school se aate waqt woh peepal ke ped ke paas ruk gaya. Us pal hawa achanak ruk gayi, jaise poora gaon saans rok kar khada ho. Patte hilne lage bina hawa ke, aur Ayaan ka dil tez dhadakne laga.

Ped ke neeche use ek purani, dhool se bhari kitaab mili. Kitaab ke cover par sirf ek shabd likha tha—SHRAAP. Jaise hi Ayaan ne kitaab uthai, aas-paas ka andhera aur gehra ho gaya. Usne pehla panna khola aur use mehsoos hua jaise koi bilkul paas khada ho. Ek dheemi awaaz uske kaan me boli—“Jo padh leta hai, woh chup nahi reh paata.”

Ayaan darr gaya, par bhaaga nahi, kyunki uska mann jawab chahta tha. Us raat Ayaan bilkul so nahi paaya, khidki ke bahar patton ki awaaz aati rahi. Subah uthkar usne apne haath par ek ajeeb sa nishaan dekha. Ussi din gaon ka ek aadmi achanak gaayab ho gaya. Logon ne baat ki, par sirf itni—“shayad kahin chala gaya hoga.”

Kuch din baad doosra aadmi bhi dikhna band ho gaya. Phir teesra, phir chautha—aur har baar gaon aur zyada chup hota gaya. Ayaan ne poochna shuru kiya, par har baar use chup kara diya gaya. “Bachche zyada sawal nahi poochte,” log kehte the.

Raat ko Ayaan ko lagta jaise ped use bula raha ho. Kitaab me naye-naye naam likhe ja rahe the, bina kisi ke likhe. Ayaan samajh gaya ki yeh shraap logon ko maar nahi raha. Yeh shraap unhe chup kar raha tha. Jo bolta, jo sawal karta, woh dheere-dheere gaayab ho jaata.

Ayaan ne sab kuch apni diary me likhna shuru kiya. Woh chahta tha koi toh sach jaane. Par jab usne diary dikhani chahi, logon ne dekhne se mana kar diya. Ped ab raat me aur zyada zinda lagne laga. Patton ki awaaz me jaise shabd chhupe hote.

Ek raat awaaz aayi—“Tum bhi un jaise ho.” Ayaan ko samajh aa gaya ki uska naam bhi likha ja raha hai. Gaon ke log safe rehna chahte the, sach nahi. Aur isi chup ne shraap ko taqat di.

Ayaan akela padta gaya. Uske sawalon ka bojh koi baantne ko tayyar nahi tha. Ped ne jaise faisla kar liya ho. Raat gehri hoti chali gayi. Ayaan ghar se nikla, peepal ke ped ki taraf.

Us raat ke baad Ayaan kabhi wapas nahi aaya. Sirf uski diary mili. Diary ka aakhri panna khula hua tha. Us par likha tha— “Sach tab tak zinda rehta hai, jab tak koi saath khada ho.”

Gaon ne diary jala di. Aur isi ke saath… SHRAAP poori tarah jaag gaya.

🩸 SHRAAP – PART 2 (Chup Ka Phailna)

Ayaan ke gaayab hone ke baad gaon ne use dhoondhne ki koshish nahi ki. Log subah uthte, kaam par jaate, aur raat ko darwaze band kar lete. Jaise Ayaan kabhi tha hi nahi.

Sirf peepal ka ped badla hua lagta tha. Uske patte raat ko zyada der tak hilte rehte. Aur hawa bina wajah ruk jaati.

Gaon ke beech ek ajeeb si khamoshi bas gayi. Mandir ki ghanti ab roz nahi bajti thi. School khulta tha, par bachchon ki awaaz kam hoti ja rahi thi.

Master ji class me bolte-bolte achanak chup ho jaate. Jaise koi shabd unke gale me atak jaata ho. Bachche sawal poochne se pehle hi ruk jaate.

Ek raat doodh wale ki cycle peepal ke ped ke paas mili. Cycle seedhi khadi thi. Par doodh wala kabhi ghar nahi pahuncha.

Kisi ne report likhwane ki baat nahi ki. Kisi ne ped ki taraf ungli nahi uthai. Sabko lagta tha chup rehna hi sahi tareeka hai.

Phir ek din mandir ke pandit ji subah nahi aaye. Mandir ka darwaza khula tha. Andar diya jal raha tha. Par pandit ji nahi mile.

Us raat peepal ke ped ke neeche ek aur naam likh gaya.

Gaon walon ne mehsoos kiya ki shraap kisi ko zabardasti nahi le ja raha. Woh sirf unhe chunta tha jo ya toh sawal karte the ya fir kuch zyada dekh lete the.

Ayaan ki diary ki baatein yaad aane lagi. Par diary ab jal chuki thi. Sach ka koi saboot nahi bacha tha.

Raat ko log sapne dekhne lage. Ek hi sapna. Peepal ka ped, aur uske neeche khade saaye.

Saaye bolte nahi the. Bas dekhte the.

Gaon ki ek aurat, jo hamesha sab par comment karti thi, achanak bilkul chup ho gayi. Uski aankhen khuli rehti, jaise woh kuch dekh rahi ho jo baaki koi nahi dekh paata.

Ab gaon me log the, par awaaz nahi thi.

Peepal ka ped ab sirf ek ped nahi lagta tha. Woh gaon ka hissa ban chuka tha. Jaise sabki yaadein usme band ho.

Aur har raat, jab hawa rukti, toh patton ki sarsarahat me ek hi baat sunai deti— “Chup rehne se hi zinda reh sakte ho.”

Par shraap aur gehra ho raha tha. Kyuki chup rehna ab dar nahi, aadat ban chuki thi.

Aur jab dar aadat ban jaaye, toh shraap ko rokna mushkil ho jaata hai.

Gaon ko ab lag raha tha ki kuch toh karna padega. Par sawal yeh tha— kya sach ke saath, ya phir chup ke saath?

Isi uljhan me gaon ne ek faisla liya.

Ek aadmi ko bulaya gaya. Jo in cheezon ko “samajhta” tha.

Aur uske aane ke saath shraap ne muskurana shuru kar diya.

🕸️🕯️ SHRAAP – PART 3 (Tantrik Baba aur Jhoota Sukoon)

Teen din baad gaon ke raaste par ek ajnabi dikhai diya. Uske baal uljhe hue the aur aankhen bahut gehri, jaise woh sirf dekh nahi raha ho, balki pehchaan raha ho. Gaon me kadam rakhte hi usne chaaron taraf nazar daali, jaise use sab kuch pehle se pata ho.

Logon ne use tantrik baba kaha. Par baba ne khud ko kabhi tantrik nahi bola. Usne sirf itna kaha ki woh “yaadon ke bojh” ko samajhta hai.

bina kisi ke bole seedha peepal ke ped ki taraf chal pada. Gaon walon ka dil zor-zor se dhadakne laga. Koi use rokne ki himmat nahi kar paaya.

Ped ke paas pahunch kar baba ne mitti uthai aur aankhen band kar li. Kaafi der tak woh bilkul hilaa nahi. Jaise ped aur baba ek-doosre se baat kar rahe hon.

Phir baba ne aankhen kholi aur dheere se kaha “Yahan bhoot nahi hai.” Gaon walon ne sukoon ki saans li.

Par agla vaakya sun kar unka khoon jam gaya. “Yahan sirf woh log hain, jinhe akela chhod diya gaya.”

Baba ne bataya ki shraap kisi mantra se paida nahi hua. Shraap tab bana jab pehli baar kisi ne sach bola aur poora gaon chup raha. Ped ne sab kuch dekha. Aur ped ne sab yaad rakha.

Baba ne kaha ki shraap todna mumkin hai. Par uski keemat hoti hai. Gaon walon ne bina pooche haan keh di.

Us raat peepal ke ped ke neeche ek anushthan hua. Mantra bole gaye. Diye jale. Hawa tez hui.

Logon ko laga kuch halka ho gaya hai. Jaise koi bojh kam ho raha ho. Ped kuch pal ke liye shaant ho gaya.

Baba ne kaha “Ab shraap bandh gaya hai.”

Gaon walon ne chain ki neend li, pehli baar bahut saalon me. Mandir ki ghanti phir se baji. School me awaaz laut aayi.

Par ek cheez nahi badli. Koi bhi sach bolne ki himmat nahi kar raha tha.

Baba raat ko phir peepal ke ped ke paas aaya. Is baar akela. Usne ped se kaha “Main tumhe sambhal raha hoon, khatam nahi kar raha.”

Us pal ek saaya ped ke andar hila. Woh pehla saaya tha. Aur wahi baba bhi tha.

Baba ne sach chupaya. Usne gaon ko bachaya nahi. Usne gaon ko aadat sikha di.

Aadat—chup rehne ki.

Kuch mahine baad baba gaon se chala gaya. Par logon ne mehsoos kiya jaise woh kahin gaya hi nahi.

Ped phir se raat ko hilta tha. Par ab log use dekhkar bhi nazar fer lete the. Shraap zinda tha. Bas so raha tha.

Aur jab shraap sota hai, toh use jagane ke liye sirf ek sach bolne wala kaafi hota hai.

Das saal baad… woh sach bolne wala aane hi wala tha.

🌑 SHRAAP – FINAL PART (Das Saal Baad)

Das saal beet chuke the. Gaon badla hua lagta tha, par sirf upar se. Naye ghar ban gaye the, purane log kam ho gaye the.

Peepal ka ped ab bhi wahin tha. Bas uske aas-paas koi nahi baithta tha. Bachche use dekh kar bhi chup ho jaate the.

Log kehte the shraap khatam ho chuka hai. Par koi raat ko akela bahar nahi nikalta tha.

Ek din shehar se ek ladka aaya. Naam tha Rudra. Research ke liye gaon ki purani kahaniyan likhta tha.

Usne peepal ke ped ke baare me poochha. Gaon walon ne pehli baar ek saath ek hi jawaab diya— “Yeh bas ek ped hai.”

Rudra ko wahi baat ajeeb lagi. Itni rehearsed khamoshi.

Raat ko jab sab so rahe the, Rudra ped ke paas gaya. Hawa bilkul band thi.

Tab patte hilne lage. Dheere… phir tez.

Ped se awaaz aayi— “Tum bhi likhne aaye ho?”

Rudra ka gala sookh gaya. Usne peeche mud kar dekha. Wahan koi nahi tha.

Par ped ke andar kai saaye hile. Unme ek pehchana sa tha.

Wahi tantrik baba. Wahi pehla saaya. Wahi Ayaan.

Sab ek jaise lag rahe the.

Ped ne kaha— “Sach likhne wale yahin rehte hain.”

Agle kuch dino me gaon me cheezein hone lagi. Ek aadmi jo sabse zyada jhooth bolta tha, subah uthta hi nahi.

Ek aurat jo hamesha sabko chup rehne ko kehti thi, achanak bolna bhool gayi.

School ke master ji, jinhe sab kuch pata tha, ek raat ke baad wapas nahi aaye.

Gaon walon ne gin-na chhod diya. Paach se zyada ho chuke the.

Rudra ne sach likhna shuru kiya. Usne Ayaan ki kahani jaani. Baba ka asli raaz samjha.

Baba shraap todne nahi aaya tha. Woh shraap ka pehla rakshak tha. Usne ped ko bandha nahi, sirf bhooka rehne diya.

Aur ab ped bhooka tha.

Gaon walon ne aakhri faisla liya. Rudra ko rokna hoga. Sach phir dabana hoga.

Us raat Rudra ko ped ke paas le gaye. Kisi ne haath nahi uthaya. Bas sabne chup rehkar use chhod diya.

Ped khud aage aaya. Subah ped par ek naya naam tha.

Das saal baad gaon phir shaant tha. Par ab shraap aur bada ho chuka tha.

Kyuki ab woh sirf ek gaon tak simit nahi tha. Rudra ki likhi kahaniyaan shehar tak pahunch chuki thi.

Aakhri scene— shehar ka ek ladka phone par yeh kahani padh raha hai. Peeche hawa ruk jaati hai.

Aur uske ghar ke paas ek naya peepal ka paudha hilne lagta hai.

SHRAAP ne gaon chhod diya hai. Ab woh kahani ban chuka hai.

Aur jo kahani padhta hai… woh kabhi akela nahi hota.
