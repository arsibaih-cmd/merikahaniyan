🕰️ 3:17 AM

By Arslan Hussain

ये कहानी पढ़ी नहीं जाती। ये देखती है कि तुम कब 3:17 पर पहुँचते हो।

📖 Puri Kahani

part 1Tum Ab Akela Nahi Ho

Tum screen par text padh rahe ho. Room shaant hai… shayad thoda zyada hi shaant. Tum scroll karte ho. Agar tum ye padh rahe ho, to iska matlab hai tum ruk nahi paaye. Tumhare phone ki roshni diwaar par ajeeb sa shadow bana rahi hai. Tum notice karte ho— wo shadow tumhari movement se thoda late hil raha hai. Tum dobara scroll karte ho. Ab peeche mat dekho. Tum nahi dekhte. Par tumhe mehsoos hota hai— jaise kisi ne tumhari gardan ke paas saans li ho. Phone halka sa vibrate karta hai. Screen par naya text aata hai— Relax. Main sirf dekh raha hoon. Tum sochte ho ye sirf kahani hai. Bas words. Lekin tumhari ungli ab bhi scroll kar rahi hai. Aur kahani ab tumse baat kar rahi hai...

🕰️ Part 2: Ghadi Chal Rahi Hai

🕰️ Part 2: Ghadi Chal Rahi Hai Tumhari nazar ghadi par jaati hai. Tum chahte hue bhi time ignore nahi kar paate. ⏰ 3:16 AM Tum wapas screen dekhte ho. Text badal chuka hai. Sirf ek minute bacha hai. Tumhara dil tez dhadak raha hai. Tumne phone lock karne ki koshish ki… par screen respond nahi karti. Scroll apne aap hota hai. Jab ghadi 3:17 bajegi, to main aur paas aa jaunga. Room ka temperature achanak thoda kam lagta hai. Tumhare kaan ke paas kisi ke chalne ki awaaz aati hai. Tik… Tik… Tik… Screen par last line blink karti hai— Ab ye kahani nahi rahi. ⏰ 3:17 AM Aur usi pal tumhe lagta hai jaise koi tumhare bilkul peeche khada hai…

🕸️ Tum palat-te nahi ho। Par kahani… ab palat chuki hai।
